Ethan Baron

PhD Student, Computer Science

NYU Courant

I am a second-year PhD student in Computer Science at NYU Courant working with Andrew Gordon Wilson. My PhD is supported by an NSERC PGS D award. Last summer, I joined Amazon SCOT as an Applied Scientist Intern working on time series forecasting.

Prior to NYU, I completed my undergraduate degree in Engineering Science at the University of Toronto, majoring in Machine Intelligence. During my undergrad, I worked on information retrieval under Scott Sanner, soccer analytics under Timothy Chan, and transportation modelling under Eric Miller. I’ve also spent 18 months as a full-time data scientist at Zelus Analytics (now Teamworks Intelligence), and in my free time served as an executive of the University of Toronto Sports Analytics student group (UTSPAN).
